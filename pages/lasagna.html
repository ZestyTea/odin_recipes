Lasagna

Cheesy non-authentic lasagna perfect for midweek

Easy to make lasagna that only uses a few ingredients and makes alot of food for the whole family

ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomato
  • basil
  • lasagna sheets
  • oil
  • vegetables

Recipe

  1. make ragu
  2. make bechamel sauce
  3. assemble the lasagna
  4. bake at 200C for 30 mins
  5. cut up and enjoy!
