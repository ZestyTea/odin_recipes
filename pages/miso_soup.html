Miso soup

<--- go back to main page

perfect bowl of miso soup for a cold winter morning

Ingredients

  • wakame seaweed
  • dried kelp (kombu)
  • water
  • bonito flakes
  • miso paste
  • soft tofu
  • sesame oli
  • scallions

Recipe

  1. Rehydrate the wakame
  2. Make the dashi
  3. Add bonito flakes
  4. Strain the dashi
  5. Whisk in the miso
  6. Finish the soup
  7. Garnish the soup and enjoy!
